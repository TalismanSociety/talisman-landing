UNLOCK THE PARAVERSE

Talisman is a web3 wallet that unlocks a universe of applications in Polkadot and Kusama.

Take control of your multi-chain assets

The Talisman browser extension allows you to safely store, send, and receive assets. Connect to any application across Polkadot and Kusama, including Astar, Acala, Singular, Phala and more.

  • Send funds across the Paraverse with ease
  • Manage multi-chain assets in one place
  • Enjoy a world-class transaction experience

Easily get started with DotSama

The Talisman web app allows you to buy DOT and KSM, contribute to crowdloans, view NFTs and more.
